Nonprofit Technology Series: Developing Tech-Savvy Organizations

ConnectVA 501 Tech Club Nonprofit Technology Series: Developing Tech-Savvy Organizations offers monthly Tech Club Clinics to help nonprofit organizations become networked by ensuring their staffs are tech-knowledgeable and well-trained. 

Tech Club Clinics are succinct, high-impact training opportunities for nonprofit professionals and volunteers to increase technology knowledge and skills so they can better support their mission. Each clinic focuses on a specific technology topic and incorporates a combination of lecture presentation and hands-on, skill-building practice.  

Attend just one or attend them all!

Clinic Format: a) Lecture Presentation b) Participant Skill-Building Practice

Cost: $25 per session

Where: Community Idea Stations | WCVE
